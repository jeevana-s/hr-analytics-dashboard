📊 HR Analytics Dashboard | Employee Attrition Analysis

🚀 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing employee data to identify key factors driving attrition and provide actionable business insights.

The dashboard helps organizations understand why employees leave and how to improve retention.

---

🎯 Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition trends

- Identify high-risk departments and job roles

- Understand impact of salary, age, and job satisfaction

- Provide data-driven recommendations for HR decision-making
  
---

🛠️Tools & Technologies

- Power BI – Dashboard & Data Visualization
  
- SQL (MySQL) – Data Analysis
  
- Python (Pandas) – Data Cleaning & Preprocessing

- --

📊 Dashboard Features

- 📌 KPI Cards (Total Employees, Attrition Count, Attrition Rate, Avg Age, Avg Salary)
  
- 📌 Attrition by Department & Job Role

- 📌 Salary-wise and Age-wise Attrition Analysis

- 📌 Work Experience vs Attrition Trend

- 📌 Gender-based Attrition Distribution

- 📌 Job Satisfaction vs Attrition (Matrix View)

- 📌 Interactive Filters (Department, Job Role)

---

🔍  Key Insights

- Employees with lower salary levels show higher attrition

- Sales department has the highest employee turnover

- Majority of attrition occurs within first few years of employment

- Employees with low job satisfaction are more likely to leave

- Certain job roles (e.g., Sales Executive, Lab Technician) show higher risk

---

💡 Business Recommendations

- Improve compensation structure for low-income employees

- Focus retention strategies on high-attrition departments

- Enhance employee engagement and job satisfaction

- Implement early retention programs for new employees
